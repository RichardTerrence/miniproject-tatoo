At PINTURA, we understand the importance of privacy and security for our customers. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it. We are committed to maintaining the privacy of your personal information and handling it in a responsible manner.

Information Collection and Use

We collect personal information when you make an appointment or purchase products or services from us. This information includes your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you provide to us. This information is used to provide the services you request and to communicate with you about your appointment or purchase. We may also use your personal information to send you marketing materials, but only if you have given us permission to do so.

Data Sharing and Protection

We will not sell or share your personal information with third parties, unless required by law or in accordance with this privacy policy. We have implemented appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect the personal information we collect from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies to enhance your experience with our website and to better understand how our customers interact with our site. A cookie is a small text file that is stored on your device when you visit a website. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may limit your ability to use certain features of our site.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with applicable laws. When we make changes, we will post the updated policy on our website and update the effective date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about our privacy practices.
