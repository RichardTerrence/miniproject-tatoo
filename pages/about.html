Discover the story behind our passion for tattoo art in the Philippines.

Our Studio

Our studio is located in the heart of Manila, Philippines and is dedicated to providing high-quality tattoo services to our clients. We have a team of experienced and talented artists who are passionate about their work and strive to create unique and custom tattoos for each and every client. Our studio is equipped with the latest equipment and is sterilized to the highest standards to ensure a safe and clean environment for our clients.

Our Philosophy

At our studio, we believe that a tattoo is a personal and unique expression of one's individuality. Our goal is to work closely with our clients to bring their vision to life and create a lasting work of art. We believe in using only the highest quality inks and needles and in following all industry standards to ensure the health and safety of our clients.

Meet Our Team

Our team of artists bring a diverse range of skills and styles to the studio. Each artist has a passion for tattoo art and is dedicated to providing a custom and unique experience for each client. Get to know our team by visiting our artist profiles and book an appointment with the artist that best suits your style and vision.

At PINTURA, we believe that getting a tattoo should be a memorable and enjoyable experience, and we're dedicated to making that happen for each and every one of our clients.
