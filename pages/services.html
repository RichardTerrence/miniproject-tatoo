MICROSHADING & MICROBLADING

Microshading is also called "Shadow Eyebrow Tattoo Technique." The inconspicuous shading technique of tattooing eyebrows was used relatively recently. The main rule of this method is the complete lack of clear contours. After the procedure, the eyebrows acquire a well-groomed appearance, as if they were lightly colored with a pencil.
